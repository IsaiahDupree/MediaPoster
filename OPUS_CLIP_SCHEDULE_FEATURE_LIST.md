### 1. Social Media Calendar
- **Visual Calendar View:** See all scheduled and past posts in calendar format
- **Drag & Drop:** Move or delete posts with one click
- **Multi-Platform View:** Unified view of posts across all connected platforms
- **Date Selection:** Choose specific dates for posting
- **Historical View:** View past posts and their performance

### 2. Schedule from Project Page
- **Direct Scheduling:** Schedule clips directly from results page after clipping
- **Platform Selection:** Choose which platforms to post to
- **Custom Timing:** Set specific date and time for each post
- **Preview Before Post:** Review clip before scheduling

### 3. Bulk Scheduler (Pro Feature)
- **Multi-Clip Selection:** Select multiple clips at once using checkboxes
- **Select All Option:** Choose all clips in a project at once
- **Clip Order Control:** Manually assign posting order (1, 2, 3...)
- **Start Time Setting:** Pick date and time for first clip
- **Frequency Options:** Define posting interval (e.g., every 2 hours)
- **Preview Schedule:** Review all scheduled times before confirming
- **AI Caption Generation:** Auto-generate descriptions and hashtags
- **Calendar Integration:** View and manage all scheduled posts

### 4. Posting Frequency
- **Exact Interval:** System follows selected time interval exactly
- **Continuous Posting:** Does not skip weekends or holidays
- **Example:** 5 clips starting Monday 9:00 AM with 2-hour interval:
  - Clip 1: Monday 9:00 AM
  - Clip 2: Monday 11:00 AM
  - Clip 3: Monday 1:00 PM
  - Clip 4: Monday 3:00 PM
  - Clip 5: Monday 5:00 PM

### 5. Post Management
- **Edit Posted Times:** Adjust timing after scheduling
- **Reschedule Posts:** Move posts to different dates
- **Delete Scheduled Posts:** Cancel individual scheduled posts
- **Pause/Resume:** Coming soon feature
