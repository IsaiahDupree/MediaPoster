# Media Requirements

## Twitter

### General Guidelines
- **Max Character Length**: 280 characters (regular), 25,000 characters (Premium)
- **Media Attachments**: Up to 4 photos, 1 GIF, or 1 video per tweet
- **Rate Limit**: 100 requests per 24 hours per user (429 error if exceeded)

### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
- **File Size**: Images ≤ 5 MB, animated GIFs ≤ 15 MB
- **Animated GIF**:
  - Resolution: ≤ 1280x1080 pixels
  - Frames: ≤ 350
  - Total Pixels: Width × Height × Frames ≤ 300 million
  - File Size: ≤ 15 MB

### Video Specifications
- **Codec**: H.264 High Profile
- **Frame Rates**: 30 FPS or 60 FPS
- **Resolutions**: 1280x720 (landscape), 720x1280 (portrait), 720x720 (square)
- **Bitrates**: Video ≥ 5,000 kbps, Audio ≥ 128 kbps
- **Audio Codec**: AAC LC
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9, 1:1
- **File Size**: ≤ 512 MB
- **Duration**: 0.5 - 140 seconds
- **Dimensions**: 32x32 to 1280x1024 pixels

## Instagram

### General Guidelines
- **Caption Length**: 2200 characters max

### Image Specifications
- **Format**: JPEG, PNG
- **File Size**: ≤ 8 MB
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 to 1.91:1
- **Width**: 320px min, 1440px max
- **Recommended**: 1:1 square for optimal display

### Video Specifications
- **Format**: MP4, MOV
- **File Size**: ≤ 100 MB
- **Duration**: 3s min, 15min max (Reel), 60s max (Story)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 to 1.91:1
- **Width**: 320px min, 1440px max
- **Recommended**: 1:1 aspect ratio

### Carousel Specifications
- **Items**: 2-20 (all images or all videos)
- **Video Aspect Ratios**: 1:1, 4:5, 1.91:1

---

## LinkedIn

### General Guidelines
- **Character Limit**: 3000 characters per post

### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: JPG, GIF, PNG
- **File Size**: ≤ 5 MB
- **Pixels**: < 36,152,320 total
- **GIF Frames**: ≤ 250
- **Recommended**: 1.91:1 aspect ratio (1200x627px)

### Video Specifications
- **Format**: MP4
- **File Size**: 75 KB - 500 MB
- **Duration**: 3s - 30 minutes
- **Resolution**: 256x144 min, 4096x2304 max
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1
- **Frame Rate**: ≤ 60 fps
- **Bitrate**: ≤ 30 Mbps
- **Recommended**: 1:1 (1080x1080px) for best display

---

## Facebook

### General Guidelines
- **Character Limit**: 63,206 characters (Graph API)

### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF
- **File Size**: ≤ 30 MB
- **Resolution**: ≥ 1080px width recommended
- **Aspect Ratios**: 1:1, 4:5, 1.91:1
- **PNG File Size**: < 1 MB to prevent pixelation

### Video Specifications
- **Formats**: MP4, MOV
- **File Size**: ≤ 4 GB
- **Duration**: 3s - 240 minutes
- **Resolution**: 256x144 min, 4096x2304 max
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1
- **Frame Rate**: ≤ 30 fps
- **Bitrate**: ≤ 30 Mbps
- **Recommended**: ≤ 15 seconds for better engagement

---

## TikTok

### Description
- **Character Limit**: 2200 characters

### Video Specifications
- **Formats**: MP4 (recommended), WebM, MOV
- **Codecs**: H.264 (recommended), H.265, VP8, VP9
- **Frame Rate**: 23-60 FPS
- **Resolution**: 360px min, 4096px max (both dimensions)
- **Duration**: ≤ 10 minutes
- **File Size**: ≤ 4 GB

### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: WebP, JPEG
- **Resolution**: ≤ 1080px
- **File Size**: ≤ 20 MB per image

## Pinterest

### Description Specifications
- **Description**: 800 characters
- **Title**: 100 characters
- **Alt Text**: 800 characters
- **URL Link**: 2048 characters

### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: PNG, JPEG
- **File Size**: ≤ 20 MB

### Video Specifications
- **Formats**: MP4 (recommended), MOV
- **Codecs**: H.264 (recommended), H.265
- **Resolution**: ≥ 240p
- **Duration**: 4s min, 5 minutes max

## Threads

### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: JPEG, PNG
- **File Size**: ≤ 8 MB
- **Width**: 320px min, 1440px max
- **Aspect Ratio**: 10:1 to 1:10
- **Color Space**: sRGB

### Video Specifications
- **Formats**: MP4, MOV
- **Codecs**: H.264, HEVC (progressive, closed GOP, 4:2:0)
- **Audio**: AAC, ≤ 48 kHz, mono/stereo
- **Resolution**: ≤ 1920px width
- **Aspect Ratio**: 0.01:1 to 10:1 (9:16 recommended)
- **Frame Rate**: 23-60 FPS
- **Bitrates**: Video ≤ 100 Mbps (VBR), Audio ≤ 128 kbps
- **Duration**: 1s - 5 minutes
- **File Size**: ≤ 1 GB

### Text and Carousel
- **Text Posts**: 500 characters
- **Carousel**: 2-20 items

## YouTube

### Video Specifications
- **Formats**: MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, FLV, 3GPP, WebM
- **Recommended**: MP4 with H.264 + AAC-LC
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 standard
- **Resolutions**: 2160p (4K), 1440p, 1080p, 720p, 480p, 360p, 240p
- **Frame Rate**: 24, 25, 30, 48, 50, 60 fps
- **Bitrate**: 8 Mbps (1080p) - 35 Mbps (4K)
- **File Size**: ≤ 256 GB
- **Duration**: ≤ 12 hours

### Image Specifications
- **Channel Art**: 2560x1440px (safe area: 1546x423px)
- **Profile Picture**: 800x800px
- **Thumbnails**: 1280x720px (640px min width)
- **File Types**: JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP
- **Max File Size**: 6 MB (channel art), 2 MB (profile/thumbnail)

### Text Specifications
- **Title**: ≤ 100 characters
- **Description**: ≤ 5,000 characters
- **Tags**: Relevant keywords only
